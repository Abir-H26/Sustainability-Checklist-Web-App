// Context

Built under pressure,
in Zurich.

Developed at the ClimateAction.tech Hackathon — a competitive event bringing together developers, designers, and sustainability professionals to prototype solutions addressing real environmental challenges.

Our team identified a gap: IT projects lack structured guidance on environmental impact. We built a GenAI-powered checklist that meets teams wherever they are in their project lifecycle — from initial design all the way through maintenance.
